About

Wizard Website Development is a Website Development consultancy that was started in 2010 (by Martin Pezet) that makes modern and creative websites that tailors to the clients needs. We have countless 5 star ratings from our clients which shows that we really value the clients opinions and thoughts on their new place and how of work.

Wizard Website Development was a company started by Martin John Pezet in the Spring of 2010 from his home. The company was started because he have had many jobs working under bosses that he didn't agree with so he thought he'd try to be his own boss which has worked out as this company has been a successful as a private consultant can be. He worked on his own for 5 years and realized that some help was needed so he recruited Billy George who helped him programme some of the best websites and systems the company has created. The company has been trying to gain ground on the market leaders, and the company has been doing that by longs hours, great time management and hard work.

A quote from On the Limit: "Very satisfactory service through the use of great customer service and very clever and innovative coding!"

Wizard Website Development shows that starting as a small consultancy is a very good way to gain entrance into the market. If are an potentional client and are interesed about our service, then email martin.pezet@wizardwebdev.com.
