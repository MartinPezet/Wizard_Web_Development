Martin Pezet - CEO/Founder

Martin Pezet is the founder of Wizard Website Development starting the company from his home as a consultant. He grew the company so much he needed to start hiring other staff.

Martin Pezet is 32 and has been coding HTML since he was 15 and has been working with Billy George for 18 Years on various coding tasks that they were up to. They went to university together at Southampton university both getting masters degrees in Software Engineering.

Martin's career started working as a Web Developer for the Virgin Group and working on their new development of website where they needed more functionality on their products.

Projects that have been carried out by Martin have been commend and picked out for many awards within the Web Development industry

Martin has expressed that he wants the company to expand into software development and AI development but thinks that he will need to increase staff significantly if that dream is to be achieved.

Billy George - Head Developer

Billy George jumped into Wizard Website Development to help his friend (Martin Pezet) to create websites for the growing number of clients that the company was taking on. This became a full time job with the company investing in a workspace in the Tring Industrial Park.

Billy had 5 years in industry before coming into the Wizard Web Development team. He had 3 jobs before joining the team here, he worked for The Virgin Group (on a email and account system), BT internet (on a new customer service system) and Natwest (by helping them improve their online banking system).

Billy George has been an influential aspect to the success of Wizard Website Development as he's made some industry leading systems that some companies have enquired about the systems because they really have been some of the best in the industry.

Email : billy.george@wizardwebdev.com
